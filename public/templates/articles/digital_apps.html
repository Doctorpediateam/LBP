Lower Back Pain – Digital Apps

There are a number of digital apps that will help you combat lower back pain. Here are our reviews of some of the most popular apps:

6 Minute Back Pain Relief

WEBSITE: http://www.6minbackrelief.com

ITUNES: https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/6-minute-back-pain-relief/id1069869410?mt=8

GOOGLE PLAY: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.tcmardoc.minutebackpainrelief&hl=en

Function: This app, backed by research, promises to help people suffering from back pain via exercises and yoga poses.

Background: They offer up simple exercises that don’t require any equipment. Their goal is to aid with mild back pain, stiff necks/shoulders, and other discomforts created by sitting for extended periods of time.

General Comments: The app comes with exercises and timers along with instructions and links to videos. There are no hidden fees.

What It Does Best: It serves up a convenient plan of exercise that you can do anywhere at any time. You can also set reminders and track activity via their activity calendar/monthly graph.

Why It’s Famous: It’s simple, free, doesn’t need Internet connectivity, and offers you quick bursts of relief from sitting down all the time.

The Drawback: The app comes with too many pop-ups that can navigate you away from the app and ruin your experience.

PostureScreen: Posture, Movement, Body Composition

WEBSITE: http://postureanalysis.com

ITUNES: https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/posturescreen-posture-movement-body-composition/id405109185?mt=8

GOOGLE PLAY: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.postureco.posturescreen

Function: It can accurately assess the status of patients/clients and be used for prescribing corrective exercises according to the screening results.  

Background: Geared for chiropractors, physical  & massage Therapists, and fitness pros who want to evaluate the posture, movement and body composition of patients with back issues.

General Comments: Useful app that sells well and has positive reviews.

What It Does Best: It comes up with solutions that encompass a healthy analysis of posture, body composition (both 2D and 3D versions available), and movement screens (such as SquatScreen and PushUp Screen) that drive proper quantifiable objective measurement and documentation with uniform assessment. 

Why It’s Famous: Once the analysis and exercise selection is done, the client receives an email with links to their private exercise portal to view both still images and streaming video of their corrective protocols.

The Drawback: The $39.99 cost. It’s $14.99 per month if you buy a yearly plan.

iRehab Back Pain

WEBSITE: http://www.dotcominfoway.com

ITUNES: https://itunes.apple.com/en/app/irehab-back-pain/id417989015?mt=8

GOOGLE PLAY: N/A

Function:  iREHAB Back Pain is a physical therapist that goes with you whenever and wherever you need it.

Background: Built by physical therapists, it asks specific questions about your pain. Based on your answers, it provides you the correct exercises and the correct time to do them. 

General Comments: It evolves with your pain; so as your needs change, simply update your answers to receive different exercises.

What It Does Best: The exercise animations with multiple angle views, lines and arrows.

Why It’s Famous: Provides ongoing support for lower back pain.

The Drawback: Sometimes crashes when you restart the questions.

No Back Pain – Instant Acupressure Self-Treatment

WEBSITE: http://www.drbargak.com

ITUNES: https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/no-back-pain-instant-acupressure-self-treatment/id596927883?mt=8

GOOGLE PLAY: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.drbargak.BackPainFree

Function: Relieve your back pain without using medications via traditional Chinese massage points.

Background: Developed by Dr. Jakob Bargak who has 25 years doing acupuncture.

General Comments: There is no danger of being hurt or infected by needles; simply massage the points gently with your own finger. 

What It Does Best: Easily finds the right massage points with full HD video clips and photos. 

Why It’s Famous: No ads! 

The Drawback: It’s $3.99.

Back Pain Relief Guide

WEBSITE: http://www.6minbackrelief.com

ITUNES: https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/6-minute-back-pain-relief/id1069869410?mt=8

GOOGLE PLAY: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=back.pain2

Function: This app offers up information about back pain.

Background: Developed by Nicholas Gabriel, you get basic images and information about back pain relief. 

General Comments: The app is nothing special but is a potential source for info.

What It Does Best: It serves up a convenient plan of exercise that you can do anywhere at any time. You can also set reminders and track activity via their activity calendar/monthly graph.

Why It’s Famous: It’s simple, free, doesn’t need Internet connectivity, and offers you quick bursts of relief from sitting down all the time.

The Drawback: The information often flashes so quickly off the screen that it’s difficult to read.
