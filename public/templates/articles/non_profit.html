American Chronic Pain Association

There are a variety of non-profit organizations you can turn to for help with lower back pain.

Website: https://theacpa.org

What They Do: They offer peer support and education in pain management.

How You Can Use Them: Their website is a great resource for information and tools on all types of pain including back pain.

Reliability: They’re a legit operation in existence since 1980.

Backcare

Website: https://painuk.org/members/charities/backcare/

What They Do: They’re a British outfit that doles out info and education to anyone affected by back pain as well as helping fund research into the causes and treatment.

How You Can Use Them: If you’re in the UK you can call their helpline 0845 130 2704 or visit their website.

Reliability: They’re a legit operation founded in 1968.

 

Arthritis & Chronic Pain Research Institute

Website: http://www.pain-research.org

What They Do: They educate people and other scientists about chronic pain by supporting particular researchers and collaborations and providing their findings.

How You Can Use Them: They fund research projects that look into treatments and therapies for pain management. Perhaps you can find something of note on their website that you can bring to your doctor.

Reliability: During the past three years alone, at least 13 publications have spread their work.

 

World Spine Care

Website: https://coverageforall.org

What They Do: There is a global charity whose aim is to improve the lives of back pain sufferers in under-serviced areas around the globe.

How You Can Use Them: They offer free yoga classes in various places that help the underserved.

Reliability: it was launched relatively recently, in 2008, by Scott Haldeman, a top figure in the treatment of spinal conditions.

United Spinal Association

Website: https://www.unitedspinal.org

What They Do: They are the largest non-profit focused on spinal injuries and diseases. 

How You Can Use Them: They have a team of trauma consultants you can talk to on their website. 

Reliability: They’re a legit operation founded in 1946 by paralyzed World War Two vets.
