What is lower back pain?

Lower back pain can be related to any number of conditions, ranging from bulged discs to post-workout soreness. Pain in the lower back is one of the most popular reasons for visiting a doctor or having to take off work. It’s also a major cause of disabilities on a global scale. In fact, the majority of people have suffered from back pain at least once in their lives. We’re happy to tell you that there are a variety of treatments and therapies that should bring relief and that surgeries are rarely needed except in the most extreme scenarios.
